Astrum

How many times have you looked at the starry sky and lost the sense of direction?

The telescope is a time machine... By looking through its mirrors and lenses, you travel to thousand, million, billion years in the past, to a time when stars and galaxies formed, overcoming entropy before eventually surrendering to it.

If any constant of nature would drift an unfathomably small fraction from its known value, these jewels would never exist.

Join us in these short meditations of awe and wonder.

The Project

Astrum celebrates the wonders of the nightsky and those who makes them accessible. It is a project by Inner Space, an italian ambient electronic music composer based in Berlin. Each art piece is composed by an image selected from Astrobin.com, the main community of amateur astrophotograpers, and a music score, generated by the background starfield and hue of the image. The first sale of each NFT will be split between the photographer (50%), Inner Space (40%) and Astrobin (10%). Every subsequent resale of the asset will recognize a 5% royalty fee to the author.
