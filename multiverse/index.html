Astrum

How many times have you looked at the starry sky and lost the sense of direction?

The telescope is a time machine... By looking through its mirrors and lenses, you travel to thousand, million, billion years in the past, to a time when stars and galaxies formed, overcoming entropy before eventually surrendering to it.

If any constant of nature would drift an unfathomably small fraction from its known value, these jewels would never exist.

Join us in these short meditations of awe and wonder.
